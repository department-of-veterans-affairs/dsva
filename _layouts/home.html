What is the Digital Service?

The Digital Service is a new technical team you’ll help build within the Department of Veterans Affairs focused on serving more than 8 million Veterans who depend on VA for benefits and services. You’ll get to work alongside world-class engineers and designers from places like Google, Amazon, and Facebook.

Become a Founding Member

The Digital Service needs a variety of roles, including software engineers, designers, and operations, with specific interest in those with experience in:

  • Building high-performing engineering teams from scratch
  • Migrating from legacy systems to modern infrastructure
  • Healthcare technology
